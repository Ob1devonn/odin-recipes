Lasagna

Description

This homemade recipe has been passed down from my father from his father and only consists of all raw ingredients. Now I'm passing down this pride and joy of ours so you can wow your friends and family.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound lean ground beef salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 (16 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • ½ pound shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ½ pound shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1 pint ricotta cheese

Steps

  1. Bring water to a boil and add the pasta. Once it's as tender to liking drain water.

  2. Preheat oven to 350 fahrenheit degrees. brown beef in large skillet and season to liking; drain. Stir in spaghetti sauce and aromatics, then simmer for 3 mintues.

  3. Mix the cheeses. Place in a 13x9 pan pasta then meat sauce until height of pan, finally coating the top with cheeses.

  4. Baked in oven for 25-40 minutes, or until top is golden brown. Serve and enjoy!
